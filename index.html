HONG KONG
CONCEPT STORE

Marvella has it first Asia Pacific concept store in Hong Kong. The shop is located in Sav Hotel in Hung Hom with a total net area of approximately 35 square meters, and had provided services since 1st of May. The shop itself is a series of bespoke artisan jewelries in a museum-like retail setting. By introducing the display cabinet in Doric column style seemingly carved from a block of Carrara Marble, art jewelry pieces ranging from necklaces to ear-rings are showcased in a series of half sculptural mannequins resembling that of Musee Paul Belmondo in France. The “columns” also consist of a watch and bracelet display whereby customers can directly try out the products without taking the art piece off the display cabinet. ​
